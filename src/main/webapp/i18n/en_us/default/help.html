MULTIPLY THE CASH

Get ready to multiply your money in the exciting Multiply the Cash game! Match Your Numbers to the Lucky Numbers and win! Reveal a multiplier symbol and win a multiplied prize instantly! Select a higher ticket cost to increase the range of available multipliers.

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

The object of Multiply the Cash is to match any Lucky Number to any Your Number to win.

At the start of the game, Multiply the Cash displays a grid of 6 Lucky Numbers and a grid of 20 Your Numbers. Reveal the numbers in any order. Or, press REVEAL ALL to reveal the symbols automatically.

When a Lucky Number matches any of Your Numbers, the associated prize is awarded. Reveal a Multiplier symbol in place of Your Number and win a multiple of the prize shown.

Play ends after all symbols have been revealed. A message appears indicating the game's outcome.

Reveal All

Press REVEAL ALL to play the game to completion automatically.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Wins display as currency.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.
